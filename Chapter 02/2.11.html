Bootstrap Grids on Extra Large Devices

Resize the browser window.

The 33.3%/33.3%/ 33.3% split is set on xlarge devices i.e. 1200px and above.

The columns will stack on top of each other, when the screen width is less than 1200px i.e. on large, medium, small, and extra small devices, it will stack (100% width).
