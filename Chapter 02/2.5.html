Bootstrap Grids on Small Devices

Resize the browser window.

The 33.3%/33.3%/ 33.3% split is set on small, medium, large and xlarge devices i.e. 576px and above.

The columns will stack on top of each other, when the screen width is less than 576px i.e. on extra small devices, it will stack (100% width).
