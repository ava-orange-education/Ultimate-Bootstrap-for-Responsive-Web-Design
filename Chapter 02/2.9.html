Bootstrap Grids on Large Devices

Resize the browser window.

The 33.3%/33.3%/ 33.3% split is set on large and xlarge devices i.e. 992px and above.

The columns will stack on top of each other, when the screen width is less than 992px i.e. on medium, small, and extra small devices, it will stack (100% width).
