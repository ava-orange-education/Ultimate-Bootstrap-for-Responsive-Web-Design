Bootstrap Grids on xx Large Devices

Resize the browser window.

The 33.3%/33.3%/ 33.3% split is set on xxlarge devices i.e. 1400px and above.

The columns will stack on top of each other, when the screen width is less than 1400px i.e. on extra large, large, medium, small, and extra small devices, it will stack (100% width).
